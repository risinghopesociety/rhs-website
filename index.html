Rising Hope Society (RHS) is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare, education, and dignity of the people of Khairpur Tamewali and surrounding areas.

Join Us Get Help
Our Purpose

Service rooted in community, driven by people.

Founded by residents of Khairpur Tamewali, Rising Hope Society works on grassroots welfare projects — food packages, shelter, health camps, education support, and relief drives — entirely powered by volunteer members and generous donors.

Registered NGO Khairpur Tamewali Community Driven

Community First

Every project begins and ends with the people of our community in mind. We serve without discrimination.
